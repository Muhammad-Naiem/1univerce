Frequently Asked Questions

1
Who is 1UNIVERSE?

We are a visionary collective composed of talented artists, musicians, and creatives who are driven by the belief that music transcends boundaries and speaks directly to the soul. Our team is dedicated to creating a world where judgment is replaced with understanding and division is transformed into harmony.

With a diverse range of talents and backgrounds, we work collaboratively to craft music that unites people and fosters connection across all walks of life. Our mission is to inspire global awareness and remind everyone that, at our core, we are all one.

2
How do we make music?

Our music creation process is collaborative and deeply intentional. For our latest release, "GAME OF LIFE" we focused on creating a classic funk nu-disco sound with rich, analog textures.

Live instrumentation was recorded over 1.5 years using analog gear, featuring a live string quartet, brass section, and funky electric guitar & bass. We used iconic equipment such as the TR-909 drum machine, OB-Xa synth, EMU SP-1200 sampler, and Roland SVC-350 vocoder, drawing inspiration from artists like Daft Punk.

3
What is "Music is Human"?

"Music is Human" is an anthem for our time, reflecting the essence of what it means to be human. This album delves into the core of human existence, exploring emotions, struggles, and triumphs that define our lives.

Each track is crafted to connect with individuals and remind them that they are not alone in their experiences. This album serves as a bridge between hearts and minds, linking people of all backgrounds.

We believe that through music, we can heal old wounds, inspire new beginnings, and create a future where love connects us all. "Music is Human '' embodies our commitment to world peace, global awareness, and the well-being of our planet.

4
What does 1UNIVERSE stand for?

We stand for healing, unity, and the transformative power of music. We are committed to creating a world where peace, understanding, and love replace division and conflict.

Our mission is to advocate for world peace and inspire everyone to find the highest version of themselves, ensuring that our impact extends far beyond the realm of music. By using music as a universal language, we aim to heal old wounds and foster a future where love connects us all.

Join us on this transformative journey as we change the game of life together.

5
How do I partner with 1Universe?

1UNIVERSE is music publishing company seeking serious partnership opportunities with:

  • Media Outlets: Collaborate on music-related features, interviews, and promotional content.
  • Film and TV Productions: License our music for use in films, television shows, and other visual media projects.
  • Sync Opportunities: Integrate our music into advertisements, video games, and digital content.
  • Talented Artists with Shared Ethical Values: Collaborate on new music projects, live performances, and creative endeavors.

To explore partnership opportunities, email us at contact@1universe.fm or use our contact button located in the footer of our website.
